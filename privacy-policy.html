Privacy Policy for Screen Annotator Chrome Extension

Last updated: December 8, 2024

Introduction

Screen Annotator ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension handles your data.

Data Collection

Screen Annotator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data processing happens entirely on your local device.

Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's local storage API to temporarily store screenshots and metadata on your device. This includes:

  • Screenshots: Captured images are stored temporarily in Chrome's local storage
  • Metadata: URL and timestamp of the captured page (stored temporarily)

This data:

  • Is stored only on your local device
  • Is never transmitted to external servers
  • Is automatically cleared when you close the editor tab
  • Can be manually cleared via Chrome's storage settings

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To capture screenshots of the current browser tab
  • tabs: To access the URL of the captured tab for metadata purposes
  • storage: To temporarily store screenshot data locally on your device

These permissions are used solely for the extension's core functionality and are not used to collect or transmit any personal information.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, advertising, or data collection services. No data is shared with third parties.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted externally, your screenshots and metadata remain private and secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the extension's data handling practices, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

This privacy policy applies to the Screen Annotator Chrome Extension. By using this extension, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.
